The Long Dark will release the first paid content

20.04.2022 | 04:32

The creators of The Long Dark talked about the current state of affairs in a realistic survival game and about their plans for the development of the game. This story was timed to coincide with the imminent anniversary of the creation of the company: Hinterland Studio Inc. was founded almost ten years ago.

During this time, the developers ran a successful Kickstarter The Long Dark campaign, released the game in early access, introduced the players to the Wintermute story campaign, remade it for the Redux edition, announced the official release, released three more episodes, and continued to develop the survival mode. And all this was done in the form of free updates.

Already this year, the developers plan to release the first paid content for survival mode. Details are not yet final, but players will have something like a season pass with a paid update period of 15-18 months. It will probably be divided into three or four separate content sets.
