Ghostwire: Tokyo Takes Two Spots on Steam's Latest Chart

25.04.2022 | 12:02

The top three on the Steam chart, where products are ordered by weekly revenue, remained unchanged: Elden Ring, Steam Deck and It Takes Two are still in the top three.

And behind them is Ghostwire: Tokyo, which has two positions in the top 10 at once: pre-orders are on the eighth line. This week, Bethesda unveiled the extended system requirements for a paranormal action movie that ended up online before it even premiered. In terms of journalists' impressions of the game, the overall reception of the game was relatively warm, although it was not without criticism. And closes the fresh chart LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga - more precisely, pre-orders of the game, which will be released on April 5th. This week, by the way, the journalists published the final previews, calling the new LEGO Star Wars a great project for fans.
