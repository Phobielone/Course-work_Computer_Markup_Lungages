God Of War : Ragnorok will be released this year. Shared with us animation director Bruno Velazquez.

29.04.2022 | 16:46

A couple of days ago, on the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the release of God of War (2018), Sony Santa Monica creative director Cory Barlog thanked the players for their support and stated that the developers have nothing to say about GoW: Ragnarök yet. This worried some fans: will the release of the sequel, scheduled for 2022, be postponed? No, they won't. Probably…

In any case, the animation director of action Bruno Velazquez (Bruno Velazquez) is sure that the studio is on time: "Ragnarök will be released this year," he answered the question of one of the users.

Recall that God of War: Ragnarök is being developed for PlayStation 4 and 5. The game will continue the story of Kratos and Atreus and put an end to the Scandinavian adventures of the heroes – the third part is not planned.
