LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga sells 3.2 million copies in two weeks, a record for a LEGO game

28.04.2022 | 11:59

The fact that LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga is doing well with sales has already become clear earlier - the success was evidenced by a large launch on Steam and a triumph in the British retail. And now Warner Bros. Games and TT Games studio revealed the total circulation of the game - 3.2 million copies.

The Skywalker Saga was not only a commercial success, but also received good press and public feedback: an average OpenCritic score of 82/100, and a 94% positive rate on Steam based on 16,006 reviews. The adventure is praised for its cute graphics, entertaining combat system, pleasant humor and a huge amount of quality content - a real gift for fans of LEGO and Star Wars!

LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga is available on PC, PlayStation, Xbox and Nintendo Switch.
