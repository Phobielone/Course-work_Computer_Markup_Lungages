Alpha testing of space shooter Marauders starts in May

21.04.2022 | 10:11

The first phase of closed testing will be held from May 4 to May 9, and in order to get access to it, you must pre-order the game on the official website. Steam is currently unable to pre-order the game.

The action of Marauders takes place in an alternative universe where the First World War has not yet ended. Many left their home planet by the 1990s, the same fate awaits our characters: to become space pirates and surf space on their own ship.

Players are waiting for a cruel world with battles for valuable resources. We are waiting for clashes alone or in teams of up to three players, and in case of loss - the danger of losing our resources. And we will increase the chances of winning by improving equipment, increasing the power of the ship, or capturing new ones from defeated opponents.
