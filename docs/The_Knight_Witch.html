Shooter, metroidvania, card battler: The Knight Witch announced

22.04.2022 | 15:24

The independent studio Super Awesome Hyper Dimensional Mega Team, known for action adventure Rise & Shine, has announced its new project. The Knight Witch is due out this year on PC and consoles, and Team17 is responsible for publishing it.

The gameplay of The Knight Witch is built on a mixture of different genres. On the one hand, we have a typical hand-drawn 2D metroidvania, in which we can freely move around. At the same time, we will be able to wield not only spells, but also a machine gun, according to all the laws of a shooter.

The Super Mega Team studio is made up of game industry veterans who have been involved in the development of Plants vs Zombies, RiME, Gylt, Moonlighter and Worms 3D.
