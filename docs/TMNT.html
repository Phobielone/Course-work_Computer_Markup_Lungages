Turtles fight all over the place in new TMNT gameplay: Shredder's' Revenge

28.04.2022 | 13:45

The network has a new gameplay videoTeenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Shredder's Revenge, which showed a couple of gaming locations. A project in the beat 'em genre and the style of the classic 1987 cartoon will send the famous Turtles to new battles with opponents. In the presented video, the heroes are trying to catch up with their nemesis Shredder, but he continues to successfully escape. In the process, the characters encounter gangsters in offices and TV studio sets, eventually meeting Bebop. And then the battles spill over into the streets of New York, where the Turtles end up in a brawl with Rocksteady. By the way, the characters in the game are voiced by the same actors from the original 1987.

The release of the project is scheduled for this summer for Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch and PC.
