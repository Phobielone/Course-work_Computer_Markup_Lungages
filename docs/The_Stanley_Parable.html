Over 100 thousand copies of The Stanley Parable: Ultra Deluxe were sold per day on Steam

21.04.2022 | 13:56

The Stanley Parable is a computer game that belongs to the visual novel and interactive fiction genres. It was originally released by Davy Rieden in July 2011 as a modification on the Source engine, but later redesigned by him and William Pugh into a full-fledged game.

Over 100 thousand copies of The Stanley Parable: Ultra Deluxe were sold per day on Steam

In the first 24 hours, The Stanley Parable: Ultra Deluxe sold over 100,000 copies on Steam. The developers from the studio told about this in their Twitter account. They thanked all those who bought the game, stream it and talk about it.

The Stanley Parable: Ultra Deluxe released yesterday on PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox one, Xbox Series and Nintendo Switch. More endings, materials, choices, and secrets have been added to the game, as well as various support features.
