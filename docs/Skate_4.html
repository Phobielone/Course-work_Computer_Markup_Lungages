Rumor: Skate 4 will sell heavily on user-generated content

27.04.2022 | 15:28

Following the leaked Skate 4 pre-alpha gameplay, details about the game itself began to appear online. Jeff Grubb, in the latest episode of his Grubbsnax podcast, said that the lucky ones who managed to play the early build were satisfied with what they saw.

According to him, EA is trying to take the best from the previous parts, so as not to disappoint the fans of the series, but at the same time intends to integrate a number of modern elements into the project. For example, an advanced system for customizing characters and their skateboards, as well as support for user-generated content. Skate 4 will even have a creative online mode that combines direct gameplay, a skate park editor and a social component. Roughly speaking, gamers will be able to team up with each other to communicate, trump tricks and create their own maps.
