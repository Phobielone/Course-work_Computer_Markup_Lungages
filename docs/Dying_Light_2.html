Dying light 2 is bought 5 million times in the first three weeks

24.04.2022 | 10:14

Although Dying Light 2 didn't get sky-high reviews from the press, the sales of the zombie action game should be great. At least all editions of the game, including pre-orders, entered the fresh Steam chart, and some of them took the top three places.

At the time of writing the news peak online Dying Light 2 stopped at around 274,983 users (two hours ago). The creation of Techland is on the fourth line in the list of the most populated games of the Valve store - behind Apex Legends, Rust and Grand Theft Auto V.

In addition, Dying Light 2's rating on Steam has gone up slightly. Shortly after the release, the players scribbled 77% of positive reviews, and now it is already 80%.
