Horror The Quarry authors Until Dawn and The Dark Pictures went to gold

23.04.2022 | 02:10

The upcoming horror film The Quarry by the authors of Until Dawn and The Dark Pictures went gold - the creators of the game announced this on Twitter.

Publisher 2K also thanked the creative talent at Supermassive Games for "bringing their next great thriller to life." In anticipation of the game, check out IGN's enthusiastic preview and developer material, which talked about how the new game develops the ideas of Until Dawn and about the features of the cinema mode and multiplayer.

The Quarry comes out on June 10 on PC, Xbox One, Xbox Series, PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5. Pre-order and the game page in Russia are not yet available, but a full Russian translation is promised.
