"Btw, this is not a battle royal" - Ubisoft announces "Project Q" pvp action. Gameplay leak

26.04.2022 | 13:43

The day before, a video with a gameplay demonstration of an unannounced Ubisoft project, codenamed Project Q, began to spread on the Web. As a result, the company officially recognized its existence and invited players to take part in testing.

More about Project Q told insider Tom Henderson (Tom Henderson). For example, Ubisoft Bordeaux is developing the game, and last year's leak in GeForce NOW revealed the existence of the game. At the moment, the action has two modes: Showdown and Battle Zone. You can learn more about them here.

Project Q is in production not so long ago, so the release should not be expected in the near future. Based on the list of platforms for testing, the game may appear on PC, PlayStation and Xbox. In addition to this action, Ubisoft is working on Pathfinder, an analogue of the royal battle, similar to the dying Hyper Scape.
